Key Principles of the Montessori Method

Individual Approach

We consider each child's abilities, pace, and learning style, helping them open the door to the scientific world.

No Grades, No Homework

We rely on internal motivation for learning. Practice takes place in the classroom, freeing up evenings for family.

Mixed-Age Classroom

Children aged 6-12 learn together, developing social skills. Younger ones look up to older ones, who become mentors.

Freedom & Responsibility

The child chooses their own activities, which develops their internal motivation and responsibility for their choices.

What Children Learn & Our Daily Routine

Broad and Deep Education

We create a holistic view of the world. Students study:

  • Russian, English, Serbian languages
  • Arithmetic and Geometry
  • Botany and Zoology
  • Geography and World History
  • Art, creativity and physical education
