A game where teenagers create their first startup ideas

An online session for teenagers aged 10–14 where they can try themselves as creators.

We explore problems around us, come up with solutions, and see how an idea can turn into a startup or business.

Join the game Message us on Telegram to find out the next date.

This is not a lesson. It’s a game.

In one session, teenagers go from observation to idea. There are no right or wrong ideas — the goal is to try, discuss, and think together.

Find a problem

Identify a real problem in the world around them.

Create an idea

Come up with a startup or business idea to solve it.

Build a prototype

Create a simple sketch or model.

Present

Share ideas and receive feedback.

For teenagers who like to create ideas

  • ask “how does this work?”
  • enjoy creating projects
  • are curious about business
  • want to try their own idea
